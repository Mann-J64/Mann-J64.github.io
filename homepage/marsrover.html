The Hall of Science Mars Rover

This is the New York City Hall of Science Mars Rover. It was made by two siblings and their dad for one of the exhibits where you can control the rover around it's enclosure. There are also two half globes, one half of the earth and the other half being mars. When you touch the earth globe, it's warm. When you touch the mars globe, it's noticably colder than the earth globe. One of my favorite experiences in the Hall of Science, it's just fun to play around with and watch it go.
